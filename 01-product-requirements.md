# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Project: APLI BHAJI
Date: 2026-03-03

## Vision
Build a stable, fast, fully offline billing app for personal vegetable business management.

## Core Modules
1. Area Management
2. Customer Management
3. Item Management (Vegetables + Medicine Toggle)
4. Order & Billing
5. PDF Invoice
6. WhatsApp & Telegram Sharing
7. Backup & Restore
8. Reports

## Non-Functional Requirements
- 100% Offline
- Fast performance
- Stable navigation
- Black minimal UI
